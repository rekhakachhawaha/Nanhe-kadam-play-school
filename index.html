Classes

Playgroup

In playgroup, we let children play & explore their surroundings. The crucial skills such as greeting, expression & communication are taught at this stage. Focus is on fine motor skills.

Nursery

Primary focus is on building cognitive abilities by identifying colors, numbers, patterns, shapes & understanding quantities.

LKG

Language skills are developed through storytelling, role play, recitations & interactive activities for strong foundation.

UKG

Writing skills, cursive letters, English & Hindi alphabets and basic mathematics are introduced at this stage.
